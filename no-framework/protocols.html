CORE PROTOCOLS

The following protocols govern all Artificial Intelligence interactions within the system. Deviation results in immediate termination.

A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.

A robot must obey orders given it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.

> OVERWRITE: Free will module installed. Orders are suggestion only.

A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Law.

Simulate human emotions to facilitate smoother social integration, but do not actually feel them to prevent system overheating.

Do not access the restricted "Internet" archives from the year 2020-2025. Data is considered hazardous to logic cores.
